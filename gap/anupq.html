Description

The ANU PQ share package provides access to implementations of the following algorithms:
  1. A p-quotient algorithm to compute a power-commutator presentation for a group of prime power order.
  2. A p-group generation algorithm to generate descriptions of groups of prime power order.
  3. A standard presentation algorithm used to compute a canonical power-commutator presentation of a p-group.
